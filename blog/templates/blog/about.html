About Smudge

Smudge is a cat that lives in Philadelphia, PA. She spent the first few years of her life living in Ann Arbor, MI and considers herself a "midwestern cat." She thoroughly enjoys milk and ice cream, though she could do without the cold.

Her days are primarily spent sleeping in various places throughout her house, including the closet. She has the best parents in the world.
